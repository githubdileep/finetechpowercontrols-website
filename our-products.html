POWER CONTROL CENTER (PCC) PANELS

Power Control Center (PCC) Panels are engineered for centralized control, monitoring, and protection of electrical systems.

KEY FEATURES

  • Centralized power distribution
  • Intelligent metering
  • High fault-withstand capability

MOTOR CONTROL CENTER (MCC) PANEL

Motor Control Center (MCC) Panels are designed to manage and control multiple motors from a centralized system.

Our MCC panels feature a robust modular and compartmentalized construction, where each motor starter unit is housed in an independent compartment. This ensures enhanced safety, faster troubleshooting, and easy maintenance while protecting motors against overload, short-circuit, and phase failure.

KEY FEATURES

  • Modular, compartment-based architecture for safe motor control
  • Comprehensive protection against overload, short-circuit & phase loss
  • Durable powder-coated enclosures for long service life
  • Withdrawable & fixed type units for easy servicing

AUTOMATIC POWER FACTOR CORRECTION (APFC) PANELS

Automatic Power Factor Correction (APFC) Panels are engineered to maintain an optimal power factor by automatically switching capacitor banks ON/OFF in response to real-time load variations.

APFC panels are ideal for industrial, commercial, and utility installations where reactive power demand fluctuates throughout the day. They are designed for long-term stability, enhanced operational reliability, and reduced overall energy consumption.

KEY FEATURES

  • Automatic capacitor bank switching based on real-time load conditions
  • Capacitor-duty contactors or thyristor-based switching options
  • Protection against over-current, over-voltage & capacitor failures
  • Detuned / filtered reactor options for harmonic-rich environments
  • Rugged, ventilated & compartmentalized construction

METER PANELS

Meter Panels are engineered for precise measurement, monitoring, and billing of electrical energy consumption across industrial, commercial, and multi-tenant facilities.

Available in single-billing, multi-bay, and tenant-wise metering configurations, Meter Panels offer excellent flexibility for diverse project requirements. Their compact modular construction with front-access design enables fast, safe, and efficient maintenance and meter replacement.

KEY FEATURES

  • Tenant-wise, feeder-wise & building-wise metering solutions
  • Modular design with lockable, tamper-resistant compartments
  • Available in both digital and analog metering configurations
  • Durable steel enclosures with high-quality powder-coated finish

AUTOMATIC MAINS FAILURE (AMF) PANEL

Automatic Mains Failure (AMF) Panels are designed to ensure uninterrupted power by automatically transferring the load from the mains supply to a generator (DG set) during a power outage. Once the mains supply is restored, the panel seamlessly switches the load back and safely shuts down the generator, ensuring smooth and reliable power continuity.

AMF panels are critical for applications where continuous and stable power is mandatory—such as hospitals, data centers, industrial plants, commercial buildings, and telecom infrastructure. These panels integrate intelligent auto start/stop logic, robust changeover mechanisms, and manual override features for complete operational reliability.

KEY FEATURES

  • Automatic changeover between mains supply and DG set
  • Auto start / stop logic for generator operation
  • Digital AMF / generator controller with detailed load monitoring
  • Manual & auto operation modes with emergency stop provision

POWER FACTOR CONTROL PANELS

Power Factor Control Panels are designed to regulate and maintain the desired power factor level in electrical networks, ensuring improved system efficiency and reduced energy losses.

Built for reliability and long-term performance, our Power Factor Control Panels support both manual and automatic operation modes. They feature high-quality power factor controllers, protective devices, and safe discharge mechanisms, making them ideal for facilities with fluctuating load patterns such as manufacturing plants, commercial buildings, and utility systems.

KEY FEATURES

  • Automatic & manual power factor regulation for optimal efficiency
  • Step-wise capacitor switching for smooth and stable correction
  • Optional detuned reactors for harmonic suppression
  • Durable MS enclosures with high-quality powder-coated finish
