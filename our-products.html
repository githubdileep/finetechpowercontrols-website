Power Control Center (PCC) Panels

Our PCC panels are engineered for centralized distribution and control of electrical power across industrial installations. They combine robustness, safety interlocks, and metering to ensure reliable operation of large motors, feeders and sub-distributions. Customizable busbar arrangements, protection schemes and front-access designs available.

  • Indoor / outdoor ratings
  • Custom busbar sizing
  • Protection & metering as per spec

Motor Control Center (MCC) Panel

MCC panels for motor management — offering flexible starter options (DOL, Soft-starter, VFD), compartmentalized construction, and easy maintenance access. Ideal for manufacturing, water treatment, HVAC and other motor-intensive applications.

Features include: segregated compartments, cable entry flexibility and integration with PLC/SCADA.

Automatic Power Factor Correction Panels

Automatic PF panels to improve system efficiency and reduce utility penalties. We supply capacitor-based and hybrid solutions with automatic switching controllers tailored to site load profiles.

  • Microcontroller / PLC based controllers
  • Step switching for smooth correction
  • Over-current & discharge protection

Meter Panels

Meter panels with precise energy, demand and power-quality metering. We design compact and multi-bay panels that can hold class-1 energy meters, CT/PT arrangements and auxiliary meters required for accurate billing and monitoring.

Options: single-billing panels, tenant-wise metering and CT chamber designs.

Automatic Mains Failure (AMF) Panel

AMF panels automate the transition between mains and generator power, enabling fast, reliable backup during outages. Panels include auto-start/stop logic, seamless changeover, engine protection interlocks and manual override functions.

Designed to integrate with DG sets and ensure smooth continuity of critical loads.

Power Factor Control Panels

Manual and automatic power factor control panels to maintain desired power factor levels across your installation. We provide rugged assemblies with easy front access, clear indication, and safe switching gear for capacitor banks.

  • Custom bank sizing
  • Safety interlocks & discharge resistors
  • Controller options for setpoint management
