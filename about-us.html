About Us

Welcome to Fine Tech Power Controls

Leading Electric Power & Control Panel Manufacturer

Fine Tech Power Controls is a trusted manufacturer and supplier of high-quality electrical panels, delivering reliable solutions for various industrial, commercial and residential applications. We specialize in designing and producing a wide range of products, including Power Control Centre Panels, Automatic Power Factor Panels, Auto Mains Failure Panels, Power Control Center Panel Boards, Meter Panels, Busducts, Power Factor Control Panels, and Power Distribution Panels.

Every product we deliver is engineered using modern manufacturing practices, advanced technology, and strict quality standards. This ensures our panels offer superior performance, long service life, and complete safety for end users. Supported by a team of experienced professionals, we strive to meet diverse customer requirements with precision and commitment.

With years of industry experience, we have become a trusted partner for companies seeking reliable electrical solutions. Our modern manufacturing facility, skilled workforce, and customer-first approach enable us to deliver projects on time while maintaining exceptional quality. Whether you need a standard panel or a fully customized system, we ensure superior engineering, easy installation, and long-term operational stability.
